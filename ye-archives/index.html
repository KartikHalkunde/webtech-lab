Kanye West

The Discography of a Visionary

The Artist. The Visionary.

Kanye West, now known as Ye, is a trailblazing American rapper, singer, songwriter, record producer, and fashion designer. One of the most influential and successful musicians of his generation, Ye's career is defined by a constant evolution of sound and style, pushing the boundaries of hip-hop and popular music.

From the soulful, sped-up vocal samples of The College Dropout to the abrasive industrial sounds of Yeezus and the maximalist grandeur of My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy, he has consistently redefined what is possible in the studio. Beyond music, his impact on fashion through his Yeezy brand and his polarizing public persona have made him a permanent fixture in global cultural discourse.

With 24 Grammy Awards and over 160 million records sold, his legacy as a creative powerhouse is undeniable, forever changing the landscape of art and industry.
